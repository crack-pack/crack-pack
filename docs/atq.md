# Antiquities (`atq`) — known gaps & assumptions

Part of [crack-pack](../README.md) · see the [status matrix](../ASSUMPTIONS.md#status-matrix).

**Status:** sheet data ✅ validated · collation 🔴 provisional.

## What's solid
- Two 11×11 sheets (common + uncommon; no rare sheet), grids checksum-validated:
  common 11×C1 + 5×C2 + 25×C4; uncommon 26×U1 + 4×U2 + 29×U3.
- The five multi-art lands (Urza's Mine / Power Plant / Tower, Mishra's Factory,
  Strip Mine) with their `(A)`–`(D)` variants.

## The gap
- The source gives **no per-sheet stripe-width sequence** for Antiquities. We
  know it's striped on 11×11 sheets, but not the widths that determine which
  cards land together in a pack.

## What we assumed
- Stripe widths **common `[3,4,5]`, uncommon `[3,4]`** — copied from Arabian
  Nights as a stand-in. The card grids are correct; only the pack *grouping* is
  unconfirmed.

## How you can help
- The **real per-sheet stripe widths**, from a box-opening video or a physical
  sheet. A single box opening is usually enough to reconstruct the widths.
- [Open an issue or PR](https://github.com/crack-pack/crack-pack/issues).
