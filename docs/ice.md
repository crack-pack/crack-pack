# Ice Age (`ice`) — known gaps & assumptions

Part of [crack-pack](../README.md) · see the [status matrix](../ASSUMPTIONS.md#status-matrix).

**Status:** sheet data ✅ all three sheets real (rare hand-transcribed) · collation 🔴 provisional.

## What's solid
- **All three sheets are real card grids** (121 distinct each). Basic/snow-covered
  lands are on a separate land sheet and don't appear in boosters, so these are
  pure card grids. Pack = 11 commons + 3 uncommons + 1 rare.
- **Common and uncommon** sheets come from The Collation Project and are
  checksum-validated. Common uses "version 1" of two shown printings.
- **Rare sheet** was transcribed by hand from a photo of the uncut rare sheet on
  [magiclibrarities.net](https://www.magiclibrarities.net/) (the Collation Project
  page has no rare-sheet gallery). It was validated programmatically — all 121
  real Ice Age rares present exactly once, no duplicates or omissions — and its
  positions were eyeballed against the sheet photo. This replaced an earlier
  randomised placeholder grid.

## Remaining caveats
- **Rare positions rest on a manual read of a photograph.** The *set* of 121 cards
  is certain, but individual card positions could be off by a cell in places. Since
  striped collation depends on exact positions, a clearer scan or box-opening data
  could refine which rares pack together.
- **Stripe cycle `[2,3,4,5]` assumed** for all sheets. The source notes variable
  collation (some boxes split the first common sheet top-5/bottom-6; width-1
  stripes observed), so the real per-sheet stripe widths are not pinned down.
